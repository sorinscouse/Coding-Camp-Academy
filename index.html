About Us

We created 'Coding Camp Academy' because we believe that everyone that has the right desire for learning to code can do it! We provide courses for the whole Web Development: Backend Development or Frontend Development paths, the choise is yours!

Our coding bootcamp could train students in any development skills, many of them changed their careers after graduating! The stats don't lie: 90% or more of our students got full-time programming jobs or freelancing positions within 6 to 12 months of graduation.

Mission

Our mission is to help students to develop their new coding skills and to make this journey as pleasant as possible!

Vision

IT industry and working in this field is really the future of our civilisation, you ca't be wrong if you choose to learn programming.

Passion

We have a passion for coding and programming in general. Our instructors just want to share their passion with others willing to learn!
